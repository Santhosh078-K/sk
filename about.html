About S.K Robotics

Robotics is not about building machines it’s about building minds."

— Sabarinathan Dhandapani

Who We Are

S.K Robotics is a pioneering educational institution dedicated to fostering innovation and technical skills in young minds. Founded by engineer and parent Sabarinathan Dhandapani, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality robotics and technology education.

We believe in "Learning by Doing," offering hands-on training in cutting-edge fields like Electronics, Robotics, AI, IoT, Embedded Systems, and Programming. Our project-based approach ensures students not only grasp theoretical concepts but also gain practical experience, preparing them for the demands of Industry 5.0.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution in India for robotics and technology education, inspiring a generation of confident and creative innovators who can shape the future through practical application of knowledge. We envision a world where every student has the opportunity to transform their ideas into reality.

Our Mission

To provide hands-on, affordable, and high-quality technical training in robotics, AI, IoT, and related fields. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical application, equipping students from diverse backgrounds with essential skills for school projects, competitive success, and future careers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.
